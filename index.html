State Exchange

State Exchange is the result of an international collaboration with students from Fulda University in Germany. The product is an e-commerce platform that allows students to exchange goods within a university environment. Students can make an account, upload items to sell, and contact other sellers through a simple messaging service.

Uno

This card game application is the result of a 10 week, 4 student collaboration to build a real-time, multiplayer, online game that integrates front-end design and layout principles, back-end routing and serving of data, and a database to persist user data, game states, and to allow for live chat capabilities.

Eating for Free
